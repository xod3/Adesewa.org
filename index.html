How to Join the
Daily Destiny Helper
Box Battle

Adesewa goes LIVE every weekday, Mon to Fri, at 5PM MST, 6PM CST, 7PM EST and 12AM GMT. Here is exactly how you can step in and be part of it.

01

Follow and Turn on Notifications

Go to TikTok and follow @sewa_hairmpire right now. Tap the bell so your phone lets you know the moment she goes live. You do not want to miss the start.

02

Jump Into the LIVE When It Starts

The moment it goes up, get in there. The community is already buzzing and Adesewa is ready. She goes live Mon to Fri at 5PM MST, 6PM CST, 7PM EST and 12AM GMT. Pick your timezone and set your reminder.

03

Send a Gift to Get Into the Box

When you gift during the LIVE, it puts you in the box. Adesewa sees you, the community sees you, and your gift goes toward real people in need through her outreach work. It is more than just a battle.

04

Battle it Out and Bring Your People

Once you are in the box, it is go time. Tell your friends, tell your followers, get them in the room cheering for you. The louder your side is, the stronger you look. Every single person in that room matters.

05

You Are Now Part of the Family

Whether you won the box or not, you showed up and that is what counts. You are part of something real now. A community that actually lifts each other up. Welcome in. 🙌🌟
